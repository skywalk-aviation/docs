Sign in Screen
========================

As a user you can sign in with your email; no password needed.
First, you enter your email address and click “Send code.” You’ll receive a 6-digit code in your inbox within seconds.
Enter this code in the app to verify your identity. Behind the scenes, your device is recognized automatically, so you don’t have to set anything up yourself.
Once the code is correct, you’re signed in and can continue using SkyWalk immediately.

After signing in, you stay logged in on that device, so you don’t have to repeat the process every time.
If your subscription is active, everything works seamlessly in the background.
If you try to log in on too many devices, you’ll be asked to remove one before adding a new one.
And if your subscription expires, you’ll simply be signed out and guided to renew.
